About Us:


Bingfurniture is an online shopping site that offers affordable contemporary furniture for every Filipino home. We give our customers the best furniture online shopping experience, with a wide selection of furniture collections and items to choose from and in-house services that cater to all of your furniture needs.

Our free Furniture Layout Design service helps new homeowners, home renovators, and developers find the right pieces of furniture that fit your space and budget. You can also enjoy hassle-free deliveries, assembly, and installation from our highly trained and dedicated staff.
